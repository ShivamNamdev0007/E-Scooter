Why Super73?


Super73 Electric Bikes are the industry standard in style and performance!

Fun for every rider!

With a bike for every rider and any condition, Super73 is the easiest get on and go
e-bike out there. With multiple levels of pedal assist and speed levels, these bikes
can be customized to changing terrains and specific riders. Don't feel like
pedaling? All Super73's have throttles installed. Throw in an easy and intuitive
app that allows on the fly changes as well as software updates and there isn't
anything you can't tackle in style.
